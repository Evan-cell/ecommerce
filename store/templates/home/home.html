    Welcome to ADILI SOLAR HUBS

    At Adili Solar Hubs Limited, we believe that our clean energy for productive use projects can eradicate poverty in rural areas we establish them at.

    about us

    Background

    Adili Solar Hubs was set up in 2019. The company was the vision of Evan Kimani, an engineer, entrepreneur, and a green energy enthusiast. It was his vision to set up cold chain management units in marginalized communities

    about us

    Objective

    The aim was to create a social business that would curb food spoilage, improve income for members of the communities, provide employment and equal opportunities to all genders. The company was set to do this using clean and renewable energy.
